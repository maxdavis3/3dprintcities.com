Urban Printing Services

01

City Planning Models

Large-format urban planning models from GIS and city BIM data. Full city blocks, transport corridor studies, and masterplan visualizations at 1:500 to 1:2000 scale. Developer and planning authority presentations.

02

Smart Infrastructure Fabrication

Custom street furniture, modular infrastructure elements, and parametric public space components via concrete and polymer 3D printing. Geometry optimized for manufacture, installation, and urban integration.

03

Urban Data Visualization

3D physical data visualizations — density maps, noise models, and wind flow studies translated into tangible printed form. Stakeholder engagement tools that outperform screen-based data presentations.

TECHNOLOGIES

Urban Print Technologies

City-scale printing demands a range of technologies — from precision resin models to full-scale concrete extrusion.

  • ● Full-color resin — city planning models at any scale
  • ● Concrete extrusion — full-scale infrastructure elements
  • ● Sandstone composite — durable public realm objects
  • ● Flexible polymer — transport and utility components
  • ● GIS and BIM data processing included
  • ● On-site robotic print capability for large structures
